Singularity is designed to bridge the gap between institutional knowledge and those who need it. Whether you are a student looking for quick answers or an administrator managing an entire organization, Singularity provides the tools you need.

## For Students 🎓

### 1. AI-Powered Chat Assistant
The heart of Singularity is its intelligent chat interface. Students can ask questions about:
- Course materials and syllabi.
- Campus policies and procedures.
- Important dates and deadlines.
- General academic guidance.

The AI provides **instant, accurate responses** based directly on the documents uploaded by your institution.

### 2. Personal Dashboard
Track your academic progress with a personalized view:
- View your registered details (Roll Number, Year, etc.).
- Access chat history to revisit previous guidance.
- Stay updated with institutional announcements.

### 3. Secure Verification
Student privacy is paramount. Singularity uses a robust verification process to ensure only authorized students can access sensitive institutional data.

---

## For Organizations 🏢

### 1. Knowledge Base Management
Administrators can easily build their institution's "AI Brain":
- **Document Upload**: Submit PDFs, Word docs, and text files for indexing.
- **Automated Processing**: The system automatically parses and prepares your data for the AI assistant.
- **Dynamic Updates**: Add new information anytime to keep the AI up-to-date.

### 2. Advanced Analytics
Gain deep insights into your student body's performance and engagement:
- **Status Distribution**: Real-time breakdown of students by academic standing (Pass, PCP, Fail).
- **Branch Performance**: Compare metrics across different departments (CSE, ECE, etc.).
- **Engagement Metrics**: See which topics students are asking about most frequently.

### 3. Member Management
Control access to your organization:
- **Invite Staff**: Add administrators to help manage the platform.
- **Manage Requests**: Approve or deny membership requests from students.
- **Role-Based Access**: Ensure users only see what they are authorized to see.

### 4. Billing & Plans
Flexible subscription models to suit institutions of all sizes:
- Manage your plan through an integrated **Customer Portal**.
- Seamless upgrades and renewals powered by Dodo Payments.
